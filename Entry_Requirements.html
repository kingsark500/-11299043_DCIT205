INSIGHT INTO ENTRY REQUIREMENTS FOR COMPUTER SCIENCE AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

Bachelor of Science in Mathematical Sciences

Mathematical Sciences Program (Actuarial Science, Biomathematics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Physics) A student must achieve the following grades in the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE):

Core: Credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science and Social Studies

Elective: Credit passes in Elective Mathematics and any other two subjects

Bachelor of Science in information Technology

Core: Credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science and Social Studies

Elective: Credit passes in any three subjects subjects
